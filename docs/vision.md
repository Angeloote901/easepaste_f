# EasePaste — Product Vision

## Overview

EasePaste is a document auto-fill application that eliminates the tedious, error-prone process of manually filling out forms and contracts. Users provide their personal information once, upload any document, and receive a fully completed file ready to sign and download.

---

## Problem

People repeatedly fill out the same information across dozens of documents throughout their lives — real estate contracts, tax forms, job applications, rental agreements, loan paperwork. This process is:

- **Time-consuming** — the same fields filled out over and over
- **Error-prone** — typos, missed fields, inconsistent formatting
- **Frustrating** — legal and financial documents are high-stakes and unforgiving

---

## Solution

EasePaste lets users store their information once in a secure personal profile. When they upload a document, EasePaste intelligently maps their profile data to the correct fields and returns the completed document — ready to review, sign, and download.

---

## Core Features

### Personal Profile
Users enter their information once into a structured profile:
- Full name, date of birth, SSN / tax ID
- Current and previous addresses
- Employment history and income
- Contact information
- Supporting documents (IDs, pay stubs, etc.)

### Document Upload
Supports a wide range of document formats:
- PDF
- DOCX (Microsoft Word)
- Real estate forms (purchase agreements, lease agreements, disclosure forms)
- Tax documents (W-9, 1040, state forms)
- Job contracts and offer letters
- Any structured form with fillable fields

### Intelligent Field Mapping
EasePaste analyzes the uploaded document and:
- Detects form fields, labels, and context
- Maps user profile data to the correct fields
- Handles variations in field naming (e.g., "First Name", "Given Name", "Applicant Name")
- Flags ambiguous or missing fields for user review

### Completed Document Output
- Returns the fully filled-out document in its original format
- Preserves original layout, signatures blocks, and formatting
- One-click download
- Optional review step before finalizing

---

## Target Users

| User | Pain Point |
|------|------------|
| Home buyers / renters | Stacks of real estate paperwork with repetitive fields |
| Job seekers | Repeated personal info on applications and onboarding forms |
| Freelancers / contractors | Frequent contracts requiring consistent personal details |
| Tax filers | Multiple state and federal forms with overlapping data |
| Anyone who moves frequently | Address updates across dozens of documents |

---

## Key Differentiators

- **One profile, every document** — enter your information once, use it everywhere
- **Format-agnostic** — works with PDFs, Word docs, and scanned forms
- **Privacy-first** — user data is encrypted and never shared or sold
- **No manual re-entry** — the entire fill process is automated
- **Review before download** — users always have final say before the document is saved

---

## Success Metrics

- Time saved per document fill (target: reduce from ~15 min to under 1 min)
- Field accuracy rate (target: >95% correct auto-fill)
- Supported document types (target: cover top 20 most common form types at launch)
- User retention after first document filled

---

## Out of Scope (v1)

- E-signature integration
- Document generation from scratch
- Multi-user / team profiles
- Notarization or legal certification
